About me
Nicolas (Nic) has been in Selling Residential and Rural properties for over 2 years and has previously had thirteen years of agribusiness experience specifically in sales and management which gives him a different perspective to the real estate industry. Nic has the ability to listen to what both the seller and the buyer are looking for and then with a lot of hard work to achieve the best possible outcome. His has strong knowledge of the local area, farming and farming practices gives him the ability to provide a truly excellent service to the rural sales portfolio. Born and raised in Goulburn, NSW and having worked within Regional NSW and interstate, he now calls Goulburn home raising his 3 children with wife Cheyenne. With a natural talent for sales and communication he is passionate and driven with a solid client focus and a desire to make not just sales but also build long term relationships. Born and raised in Goulburn and then working in regional NSW and interstate Nic again calls Goulburn home. When he's not out selling properties Nic can usually be found doing something fun outdoors with his wife, Cheyenne and 3 young children.
